How it works

01

Pay once, get your API key

After checkout, you'll receive an email with your API key (plg_live_...). Save it — it's shown once.

02

Tell your AI you're a member

At the start of a session, paste your key. The AI stores it and uses it automatically when you generate a label.

03

Generate your label as usual

Say "PLGen this" or "generate my provenance label." The AI posts to the registry and returns your permanent URL and badge.

04

Embed the badge anywhere

Drop the HTML badge into blog posts, READMEs, portfolios, or anywhere on the web. It links back to your permanent record.

FAQ

What happens after I pay?

You'll get an email within seconds containing your API key and setup instructions. No account to create — the key is your identity.

What if I lose my API key?

Email shelton@empathylab.io and we'll issue a new one. Your label history is preserved.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel in Stripe and your key stays active until the end of the billing period. Your existing labels and URLs remain permanently.

Do free-tier labels disappear?

No — they stay in the registry. They just don't have public viewer pages. Upgrade anytime and your label history carries over.

Can I use this with any AI?

Yes. Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini — anything that can follow instructions and make an HTTP request. The session prompt works with all of them.
